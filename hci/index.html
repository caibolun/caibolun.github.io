Non-Contact Human Computer Interaction (NC-HCI) System

Bolun Cai @ NGHCI Lab

South China University of Technology

Abstract

NC-HCI system offers touch-free control over the home and it’s devices, enabling interactions which are simple and immediate. By detecting the user’s hand motions system enables seamless interaction with different devices, independently of the existing control interface, and with the detection of predefined hand signs (such as “grab”, “shake” gesture), system provides the quickest and most convenient way to control distinct features of the device.
